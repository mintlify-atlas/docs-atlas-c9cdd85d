---

Xplorer is a software editor built specifically for owners of the **Oberheim Xpander** and **Matrix-12** synthesizers. Unlike generic editors with rows of identical sliders, Xplorer gives you an at-a-glance overview of all 226 parameters in a single patch — including a full view of the Modulation Matrix.

You can edit sound simultaneously from multiple sources: your computer mouse, a hardware MIDI controller, your DAW, or directly on the synthesizer itself. Xplorer always shows you in real time which parameter is being changed and by how much.

## What "real-time and bi-directional" means

Every change you make in Xplorer is sent immediately to the synthesizer's edit buffer with minimal latency. The reverse is equally true: when you turn a knob on your Xpander or Matrix-12, Xplorer updates automatically. You never need to reload a patch or resynchronize the two — the software and hardware stay in lockstep at all times.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Full parameter access" icon="sliders">
    Edit all 226 parameters of a single patch, including VCOs, VCF, VCA, 5 LFOs, 5 envelopes, 4 ramps, 3 tracks, and the Modulation Matrix.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bi-directional MIDI sync" icon="arrows-left-right">
    Turn a knob on the synth and Xplorer updates. Click in Xplorer and the synth updates. No reloads needed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MIDI automation" icon="microchip">
    Assign any MIDI CC to any parameter, including filter modes. Compatible with hardware controllers and DAW automation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Patch file management" icon="floppy-disk">
    Load and save individual patch files, extract patches from SysEx bank dumps, or back up and restore the entire synthesizer memory.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Copy / paste pages" icon="copy">
    Copy all parameters from one TRACK, ENV, LFO, or RAMP page and paste them to another (e.g., copy ENV1 to ENV3).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Patch randomizer" icon="shuffle">
    Generate an entirely new patch from random values, with controls over VCO waveforms, envelope shapes, detune, and modulation routing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bulk patch operations" icon="database">
    Retrieve all 100 single patches from the synthesizer at once, or send a full all-data-dump backup and restore.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auto-scaling UI" icon="display">
    The interface scales automatically to your system's default font size — readable on any display from 1280×900 upward.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
